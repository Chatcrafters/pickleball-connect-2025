Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

Pickleball Connect / WPC Series Europe
Email: info@pickleballconnect.eu
Website: www.pickleballconnect.eu

2. Data We Collect

When you register for a tournament or check in, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Tournament Data: Team assignment, shirt size, player photo
  • Communication Preferences: Language, WhatsApp opt-in, newsletter opt-in
  • Technical Data: Check-in timestamps, device information

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
  • Manage your tournament registration and participation
  • Send you match schedules, court assignments, and results via WhatsApp (if opted in)
  • Create player passes and team rosters
  • Inform you about future tournaments and clinics (if opted in)
  • Improve our services and event organization

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary for your tournament participation
  • Consent: WhatsApp updates and marketing communications (optional)
  • Legitimate interest: Event organization and service improvement

5. WhatsApp Communication

If you opt in to WhatsApp updates, we will send you:

  • Match schedules and court assignments
  • Real-time updates during the tournament
  • Results and standings
  • Important announcements

You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any message or contacting us.

6. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Tournament partners: Venue operators, officials (limited to necessary information)
  • Service providers: Twilio (WhatsApp), Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting)
  • Team captains: Your captain can see team roster information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Retention

  • Active players: As long as you participate in our events
  • Tournament records: 3 years for statistical and historical purposes
  • Marketing contacts: Until you unsubscribe

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent: At any time for optional processing
  • Object: To processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@pickleballconnect.eu

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), secure cloud infrastructure, and access controls.
